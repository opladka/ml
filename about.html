About ML Digest

Depth Over Hype

We write independent, mathematical, and algorithmic walkthroughs of breakthroughs in AI and Machine Learning.

Our Mission

ML Digest was founded in 2026 to bridge the gap between superficial tech journalism and dense academic literature. As language models, diffusion systems, and specialized accelerators evolve at speeds unprecedented in computer science, engineers need clear, code-first explainers that don't skimp on the math.

Every article we publish is peer-reviewed by our small team of practitioners, verified with executable PyTorch/JAX snippets, and visualised using interactive components. We believe that understanding transformer blocks, score-matching objectives, and routing functions shouldn't require decyphering jargon.

Editorial Standards

  • No Sponsored Content: We do not publish promotional material for foundation model providers or chip hardware startups.
  • Visual Explanations: Every conceptual model must include an interactive widget or vector illustration representing the mechanics.
  • Reproducible Code: Code segments represent complete blocks that can be pasted directly into training pipelines.
